Geometry · Lesson 8 · sim

Drag a corner. Watch it reclassify — and re-measure.

Drag any gold corner (A, B, C, D — in order around the shape). The reading checks whether opposite sides are parallel, whether all four sides are congruent, and whether the corners are right angles — each within a small drag tolerance, so an almost-perfect square still reads as a square. Perimeter is the sum of all four sides, in grid units (one grid square = 1 unit).
