Rotate the line. Watch all four angles at once.

Drag the gold handle to rotate the violet line through the vertex V. The gray line stays fixed. Watch ∠1–∠4: the two gold-arc angles (∠1, ∠3) always match, and so do the two green-arc angles (∠2, ∠4) — those are the vertical pairs. Any two angles that sit side by side always add to 180° — that's a linear pair.
