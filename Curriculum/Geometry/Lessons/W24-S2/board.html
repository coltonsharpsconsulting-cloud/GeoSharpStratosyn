Square fold, then a second real-world check

  • Square Fold — take a square sheet, measure and record its side length (the leg), then fold it exactly corner-to-corner along the diagonal to create two 45-45-90 triangles.
  • Unfold and measure the diagonal crease directly (the hypotenuse) with your ruler.
  • Set-Square / Second Object — measure the two legs and the hypotenuse of a real 45-45-90 set-square or any object with a right-isosceles corner.
