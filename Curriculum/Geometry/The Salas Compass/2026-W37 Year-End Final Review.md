# Year-End Final — Review

Thirty lessons, three units, one exam ahead. This week isn't new content — it's the whole year laid back out on the table, one unit per session: Unit 01 (foundations, angles, polygons, area, triangles), Unit 02 (circles, solids, radicals, Pythagorean theorem), Unit 03 (special triangles, proofs, similarity, transformations, trig).

The point isn't to relearn anything — it's to make sure everything you already know is *reachable* under exam conditions, mixed together instead of one lesson at a time.

## Words to own
- **Cumulative** — covering everything, not just the most recent lesson.
- **Mixed practice** — problems that jump between topics on purpose, the way a real final does.
