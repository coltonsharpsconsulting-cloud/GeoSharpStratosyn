# Portfolio Show & Transcript Close

Forty weeks ago you drew one dot on a page and defended that it marked an exact spot. This week, that dot has grown into four capstones, thirty Lesson Tests, and a notebook full of builds. This is the week all of that gets gathered, shown, and closed out.

Three sessions: **Assemble** the Portfolio — find every artifact from the year. **Showcase** — present your best builds out loud. **Transcript Close** — review mastery across all 30 lessons and confirm the record that goes on your transcript.

## Words to own
- **Portfolio** — the tangible evidence: photos, CAD files, write-ups, from every build this year.
- **Transcript** — the official record: course title, credit, and grade.
- **Mastery record** — the Emerging / Proficient / Mastered rating that's the real measure of the year, not just a letter grade.

## Before Session 1
Walk through this notebook, page by page, from Week 1 to now. Flag every build you remember making — you'll need the full list on Day 1 of this week.
