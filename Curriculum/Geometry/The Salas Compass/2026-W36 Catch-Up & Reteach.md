# Catch-Up & Reteach

This is the last buffer week of the year. Unit 03 — eleven lessons, special right triangles through the six trig ratios — is done, and the Year-End Final is two weeks away. Whatever's still shaky needs to get fixed now, while there's still time to fix it slowly instead of cramming it.

Same shape as Week 23: **Diagnose** which of Lessons 20–30 still feels shaky, **Reteach** the one that needs it most, then **Reassess** to prove the fix held — this time with the Final in mind, not just the next lesson.

## Words to own
- **CPCTC** — Corresponding Parts of Congruent Triangles are Congruent — what you get to conclude once two triangles are proven congruent.
- **AA similarity** — two congruent angle pairs are enough to prove two triangles similar.
- **Mastery-gated** — you don't move on because the calendar says so; you move on because you've actually got it.

## Before Session 1
Look back honestly at Lessons 20–30 — the special right triangles, the proofs, similarity, transformations, trig. Which one lesson would you least want to see cold on the Final right now? Bring that answer, and your reasoning, to Session 1.
