# Catch-Up & Reteach

No new lesson this week. Unit 02 is behind you — nine lessons, circles to radicals to the Pythagorean theorem — and Unit 03 is about to start. This week exists so that gap doesn't just ride along into special right triangles.

The rhythm is the same shape as always, aimed differently: **Diagnose** which of Lessons 11–19 still feels shaky, **Reteach** the one that needs it, then **Reassess** to prove the fix actually held. Mastery is rebuilt, not re-rolled — you don't get a fresh average, you get the highest honest demonstration.

## Words to own
- **Diagnostic** — an honest look at where you actually stand, using test scores and checkpoints, not a guess.
- **Reteach** — going back to the concept note and Lesson Practice pages for the specific idea that didn't stick.
- **Reassess** — a fresh mastery check, after the fix, to confirm it holds.

## Before Session 1
Look back at your Lessons 11–19 — the Lesson Tests, the S3 checkpoints, how the practice pages felt. Come with an honest guess at which one lesson needs a second pass, and why.
