# What I Need From You

[[Records|← your surface]]  ·  [[Home|door]]

> *Hi — the whole course is already built and waiting: units, weeks, boards, worksheets, the mastery records, all of it. What's below is the short list of real details that turn it from a course into **Stephen's** course — his name on the transcript, the real dates on the calendar, the passphrase only you hold. Fill in what you can; anything you skip for now, we can add later. Items marked ⭐ are the ones the build genuinely can't run without.*

---

## 1 · Stephen's basics

- ⭐ **Full name, exactly as it should read on a transcript** — first, middle (if any), last.
- **Current grade level** — 9th, 10th, etc.
- **Birthdate** *(optional — for records only, not used anywhere in the course itself)*.

## 2 · The schedule

- ⭐ **Start date** — the Monday (or whichever day) the course begins. The week generator stamps every single week's dates from this one date, so it has to be right before anything else can be dated.
- ⭐ **Which three weekdays are the in-person sessions** — e.g. **Mon / Wed / Fri**, or Tue/Thu/Sat, whatever fits your household.
- **How long a session runs** — default is **~1 hour**; tell me if yours should run shorter or longer.

## 3 · Credit & records

- **Is this for a transcript?** — the course is built to produce **1.0 Geometry credit** on completion; confirm that's the goal.
- ⭐ **Letter grades, or mastery-only?** — the records track mastery natively as **Emerging / Proficient / Mastered**; say whether you also want a letter-grade rollup for the transcript, or mastery-only is enough.
- **Homeschool umbrella / state reporting** — any specific requirements (a covering umbrella school, a state portfolio format, particular language on the transcript) I should match?

## 4 · Tools on hand
*(this shapes what the Build sessions can actually do)*

- **A 3D printer** — do you own one, or have access to one?
- **FreeCAD installed** — is it already on Stephen's computer? (Free, open-source — I can walk through installing it if not.)
- **Measuring tools** — a tape measure, and calipers if you have them?
- **The Math-U-See Geometry workbook + test booklet** — in hand and ready to go?

## 5 · The answer-key passphrase

- ⭐ **A word or phrase** you'll type to reveal the teacher answer keys hidden inside the worksheets. This stays private to you — it gets baked into the worksheets when they're built, and Stephen never sees it.

## 6 · Contact

- **The best family email** to reach you between sessions.

---

*Once the start date and session days are set, the week generator re-runs and every week note gets stamped with its real dates. Once the passphrase is in, it's built straight into the worksheets. That's the whole list — send back what you have, and I'll take it from there. — Colton*
